# Folio

## Overview
Folio is a visual portfolio management application designed to help users track, evaluate, and expand their investment portfolios in a clear and intuitive way.

The platform focuses on simplifying portfolio oversight by providing a visual representation of assets, financial performance, and incoming opportunities.

Folio aims to reduce the complexity of managing multiple assets by combining portfolio tracking, financial insights, and collaboration tools into a single interface.

---

## Core Concept
Instead of relying on spreadsheets or fragmented tools, Folio provides a centralized visual system where users can see their entire portfolio at a glance.

The system emphasizes visualization, mapping, and structured asset organization to make decision-making easier.

A key design inspiration is a tile or map-style interface that allows users to quickly understand the state of their assets.

---

## Primary Use Case (Real Estate Focus)
The initial version focuses on real estate portfolios.

Users can visualize properties across different lifecycle stages:

- On market
- Incoming acquisitions
- Under renovation
- Active cashflowing rentals
- Sold or exited assets

Each property acts as an individual asset node containing financial data and operational details.

---

## Portfolio Visualization
The portfolio is displayed through a tiled or map-based interface.

Assets can be grouped and categorized visually based on status or type.

Example structure:

Portfolio
    → Property tiles
        → Status indicator
        → Financial summary
        → Ownership details
        → Performance metrics

This visual layer provides a quick overview of portfolio health and distribution.

---

## Financial Tracking
Each asset contains financial information such as:

- purchase price
- liabilities
- renovation costs
- rental income
- operating expenses
- net cashflow

Users can view aggregated portfolio metrics including:

- total asset value
- portfolio cashflow
- total debt
- equity distribution

The goal is to provide simplified financial visualization rather than full accounting functionality.

---

## Incoming Opportunities
Users can track potential additions to the portfolio.

Example pipeline:

Lead
Under analysis
Offer submitted
Under contract
Incoming asset

This allows investors to manage both existing holdings and future acquisitions within the same system.

---

## Collaboration
Folio allows users to invite collaborators to their portfolio.

Possible roles include:

- investment partners
- team members
- property managers
- lead managers

Collaborators can view portfolio data or manage specific assets depending on their permissions.

---

## Key Goals
- Simplify portfolio tracking
- Provide clear visual insights
- Centralize asset information
- Enable collaboration between stakeholders
- Help users evaluate and grow their portfolios

---

## Long-Term Vision
Folio evolves into a visual operating system for asset portfolios where users can manage acquisitions, monitor performance, and collaborate with partners through a unified platform.

While the first version focuses on real estate, the system could expand to support other asset classes in the future.
